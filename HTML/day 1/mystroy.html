Theni, located in the Western Ghats of Tamil Nadu, is a lush and tranquil district known as "Earth’s Hidden Paradise," offering waterfalls, tea estates, wildlife, and rich cultural heritage Theni, often called the "Second Manchester of South India" for its thriving cotton trade It is blessed with verdant landscapes, rolling hills, and numerous plantations of cardamom, coffee, and tea. The district lies at the foothills of the Western Ghats and includes places like Meghamalai, Kurangani, and Bodi Mettu, which are renowned for their green covers, scenic vistas, and trekking opportunities
