Welcome to my native district!

i am from theni district

located in the Western Ghats of Tamil Nadu

theni is often called the "Second Manchester of South India"

It is blessed with verdant landscapes, rolling hills
In there has numerous plantations of cardamom, coffee, and tea

The district lies at the foothills of the Western Ghats and includes places like Meghamalai, Kurangani, and Bodi Mettu, which are renowned for their green covers, scenic vistas, and trekking opportunities
